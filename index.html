About East Side Diner

Welcome to East Side Diner, where the spirit of the 1950's comes alive! Located on East Main Street in Welland, our authentic American diner has been serving the community with classic comfort food, friendly service, and that nostalgic atmosphere that takes you back to the golden age of diners.

From our checkered floors to our chrome fixtures, every detail has been carefully crafted to transport you to a simpler time when life moved a little slower and food was made with love.
